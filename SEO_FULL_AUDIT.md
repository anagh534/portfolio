## Global Speed & Technical Audit
- **Core Web Vitals:** Addressed globally via `BaseLayout.astro` and `astro.config.mjs`. CSS is completely inlined (`inlineStylesheets: "always"`), and Google Analytics is deferred to interaction, eliminating render blocking and unused JS.
- **Cloudflare Actions:** Ensure "Rocket Loader" and "Email Obfuscation" are disabled in Cloudflare to prevent remaining third-party JS warnings.
- **Trailing Slashes:** Globally enforced via Astro config (`trailingSlash: "always"`). Canonical URLs are verified to include trailing slashes.
